# Social Media Digital Ambassador Checklist

| **Standard** | **The "Do" List** | **The "Don't" List** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Identity** | Keep your LinkedIn updated with your latest VIT projects. | Don't use unprofessional profile pictures on academic platforms. |
| **Privacy** | Audit your "Tagged" photos regularly to maintain a clean image. | Don't share location data or "check-ins" in real-time for safety. |
| **Truth** | Use tools like Google Lens to verify an image before sharing it. | Don't engage with "Rage-Bait" or unverified viral news. |
| **Security** | Use a password manager to keep your student credentials unique. | Don't ever share an OTP, even if the person "claims" to be faculty. |
| **Network** | Connect with VIT Alumni and industry leaders politely. | Don't spam recruiters or peers with unsolicited messages. |
